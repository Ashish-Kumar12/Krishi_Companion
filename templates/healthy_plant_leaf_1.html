Here are some imformation which will help you in taking care of plants :
  • Yellow-green color of older leaves, especially the tips and center vein, indicates nitrogen deficiency.
  • Purplish or dark green color with dying leaf tips indicates phosphorus deficiency.
  • Wilted or burnt looking leaves and sometimes yellowing between the veins indicates potassium deficiency.
These three elements are the easiest to obtain because they are the elements found in plant food that can be purchased at any nursery.
